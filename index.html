About Me

As a student at the University of Waterloo for Systems Design Engineering, I have been given many opportunities to develop my technical skills and abilities. Through the university's cooperative program I have worked with start ups that range from mobile retail management to sports science and biometrics. I have had the privilege of working with several teams with advanced technological knowledge. As my skills have started to sharpen and mature I have started to focus them towards human factors and human and computer interaction. I have become very interested in bridging the gap between the physical world and the digital. My personal research has taken me to the realms of natural gesture languages and intuitive, seamless interactions with digital systems. Through my final years at the university and my remaining cooperative terms, I will concentrate on ways to bring real world information into the digital world in meaningful and innovative ways.
